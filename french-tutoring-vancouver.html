1. Grammar, Oral communication, Reading

French tutoring Vancouver

French is a different language than English, in that the grammar is much more complex. Words are divided into masculine and faminine, and the usage will vary depending on the pronoun and other factors. Tense, verb usage are all different. Students from G7-8 level will need to build a good foundation and prepare for grade 9, in which the curriculum is much more demanding.

Students in grade 9 french are required to perform various communication with a good usage of the language. This includes writing short stories (in french), delivering a presentation or listening and responding to an everyday conversation in French.

This pose challenges to a lot of students and unless they have a firm understanding and usage of the language, it will not be easy for them.

QE French tutors will focus on the fundamentals - the grammar, pronunciation and build the skills on top, so that our students will be able to handle and excel in high school French.

2. Writing French short stories and passages (Grade 7-9 level)

French tutoring Vancouver

Writing paragraphs and short stories are part of the syllabus for senior elementary and G9 core French. The challenge is to understand and get familiar with the grammar, otherwise the student will fall into the trap of writing the paragraphs in English and trying to translate it to French. This will be a mistake. QE French tutors will guide our students in the right approach, to think in French and apply the different grammar rules. Through practice, students will understand the different and this is the only proper way to learn French

French tutoring Vancouver

In high school English courses, students are introduced to challenging literature. Examples include The Great Gatsby (grade 12), 1984 (grade 11), Animal Farm (grade 9), MacBeth (Grade 11), to name a few. Each of these books have themes that require indepth understanding. When you write your essay on these literature, you will need to include your analysis on these themes on your arguments. This is part of the application / thinking marks that covers 25-30% of your grades. Therefore, it is important for you to fully understand and analyze the text. QE English tutoring Vancouver provide comprehensive lessons on these. With good explanation, you will be guided through these difficult literature and perform well on your essays and exams.
